Here's the list of books that I am reading and have read:

## Reading

- Thinking Fast and Slow - Daniel Kahneman
> This is a book with full of insights from various studies about How Human Brain works, its quirks, behaviours and the impact in decision making. As said in the cover, have to read it chunk by chunk slowly.

- சங்க சித்திரங்கள்
> By Jeyamohan. Always wanted to explore the Sangam Literature Corpus and this is a book to introduce laymans of Tamil like me to read those.

- How To Get Whatever You Want!  - Kopmeyer
> It's a motivational book, which highlights the importance of ASKING OTHERS.

- அவள் செய்த சிற்பம் 
> a Telugu Novel written by Yandamuri Veerendranath translated to Tamil. It's a very good novel (apart from some intercepts) with a blend of Science Fiction and Human Psychology. Science Fiction depiction may not be like that of Sujatha, but still its good and the Human Behaviour analysis is ultimate also it kinda motivates and "awakens" the reader as well. Actually I was searching for his books to gift my father, as he likes the books written by him (so bought him "அந்தர் முகம்"), I found this book.

- The 100$ Startup - Chris Guillebeau

- The Bed of Procrustes - Nicholas Taleb
